<p><strong>zxsp</strong> is a simulator for the historic Sinclair ZX home computer families.</p>

<p>Supported machines:</p>

<ul>	<li>ZX Spectrum 16k, 48k, 128k, +2, +2A and +3.</li> 
	<li>Spanish and French versions. Brazilian TK90X and TK95. Portuguese TK2048.</li> 
	<li>TC2068, TS2068 and U2086</li>
	<li>ZX80, ZX81, TS1000, TS1500 and TK85</li>
	<li>Jupiter Ace</li> 
</ul> 

<p>Features:</p>

<ul>	<li>Multiple instances</li> 
	<li>Fullscreen mode</li> 
	<li>Excellent stereo sound</li> 
	<li>Precise screen and border effects</li> 
	<li>Virtual tape recorder, keyboards and extensions</li> 
	<li>Save highres and flashing Gif screenshots and record Gif movies</li>
	<li>Integrated debugger</li>
	<li>Instant-load and save tape files</li> 
</ul> 

<p>Supported file formats:</p>

<p>.sna, .z80, .ace, .tap, .tzx, .80, .81, .o, .p, .wav, .aiff, .rom, .dck, .dsk, .img, .iso, .hdf, .asm and .scr files</p>

<p>Supported real-world machinery:</p>

<ul> 	<li>Load from and save to real-world Specci or cassette recorder</li> 
	<li>USB joysticks and game pads</li> 
</ul> 

<p>Supported virtual peripherals:</p>

<ul> 	<li>Tape recorder</li>
	<li>Misc. joystick interfaces</li> 
	<li>Built-in joystick ports</li> 
	<li>Interface 2 for joysticks and rom cartridge </li> 
	<li>Kempston mouse interface</li> 
	<li>DivIDE hard disc interface</li>
	<li>SPECTRA video interface</li> 
	<li>Third party AY sound modules for the ZX81 and ZX Spectrum</li> 
	<li>Lenslok</li>
	<li>Currah ÂµSpeech</li>
	<li>Romantic Robots Multiface 1, 128 and +3</li>
</ul> 

<p>Integrated debugger:</p>

<ul> 	<li>Load assembler source files</li> 
	<li>Single stepper</li> 
	<li>Z80 and AY sound chip register display & edit</li>
	<li>Memory view & edit and disassembly & inline assembler</li>
	<li>Breakpoints for exec, read or write access</li> 
	<li>Video beam position indicator</li> 
</ul>
